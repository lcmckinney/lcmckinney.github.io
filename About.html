UPBRINGING

I was born and raised in the small town of Claremore, Oklahoma. I am the youngest among my three siblings. Both my brothers also studied at OU's Price College of Business. One majored in Accounting, the other in MIS. Unlike them, I couldn't choose just one.

Originally I wanted to become a math educator because I absolutely loved the logic and absoluteness of math. Yet, I soon switched my major to Business Management after working at my first job and thinking "I could run this place better."

Then, after a couple years at my local university I transferred to my dream college, The University of Oklahoma. Here, I took my first accounting class and didnt understand it at first, but quickly realized it was absolutely for me. I picked up a minor in MIS after taking my first MIS class, but as I took more classes, I became more and more intrigued, and the rest is history

NOW
